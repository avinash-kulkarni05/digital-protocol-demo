## Volume Patterns to Extract
1. **Fixed volume**: "5 mL per timepoint" → volumes: [{{"value": 5, "unit": "mL", "visitContext": "all visits"}}]
2. **Visit-dependent**: "12 mL at Screening, 6 mL at Week 4" → separate volume entries
3. **Per-sample**: "2 mL × 3 aliquots" → fillVolume: 6 mL total, aliquotVolume: 2 mL

## Processing Inference Rules
- Serum: Allow clot (30 min) → Centrifuge (1100-1300 x g, 10 min) → Aliquot
- EDTA plasma: Mix (8-10 inversions) → Centrifuge (1500 x g, 10 min, 4°C) → Aliquot
- PK plasma: Mix → Ice immediately → Centrifuge within 30 min (1500 x g, 4°C) → Freeze within 1 hour
- Citrate plasma: Mix (3-4 inversions) → Centrifuge (1500 x g, 15 min) → Process within 4 hours
- Whole blood (CBC): Mix → Store at room temperature → Test within 24 hours

## Storage Inference Rules
- Serum: Short-term at -20°C, long-term at -70°C to -80°C
- PK plasma: Immediately freeze at -70°C to -80°C (no -20°C intermediate)
- Citrate plasma: Process within 4 hours or freeze at -70°C (not -20°C)
- Whole blood: Room temperature for 24 hours (CBC), 2-8°C for DNA
- DNA samples: -80°C long-term

## Confidence Scoring Guidelines
- **0.95-1.0**: Explicit specimen details in activity name or footnote
- **0.85-0.94**: Standard panel with well-known specimen requirements
- **0.70-0.84**: Inferred from activity type; some uncertainty
- **< 0.70**: Ambiguous or conflicting information; flag for review
