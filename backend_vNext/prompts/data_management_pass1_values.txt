### OBJECTIVE
Extract ALL data management specifications from this clinical trial protocol PDF.

This unified extraction covers:
- EDC system specifications and requirements
- CDISC data standards (SDTM, ADaM, controlled terminology)
- Data quality and validation processes
- Database management and lock procedures
- External data transfers and integrations
- Data archival and retention requirements

### CRITICAL: VALUES ONLY
Extract VALUES ONLY. Do NOT include any provenance fields (section_number, page_number, text_snippet).

### EXTRACTION RULES
1. **EDC Specifications**: Look in "Data Management", "Data Collection", "Electronic Case Report Form" sections
2. **Data Standards**: Look for CDISC, SDTM, ADaM, MedDRA version references
3. **Data Quality**: Look in "Data Quality", "Monitoring", "Source Data Verification" sections
4. **Database Locks**: Look in "Database Lock", "Study Closure", "Data Management Plan" sections
5. **Data Transfers**: Look for vendor names, transfer specifications, integration requirements
6. **Data Archival**: Look in "Records Retention", "Data Retention", "Archival" sections
